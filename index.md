# Analysis of the Efficacy of Pre-Lecture Videos and Livestreaming in COMP 110

- Score 7: 33.5%
- Score 6: 16.9%
- Score 5: 22.3%

### Additional Livestreams

- Score 5: 19.7%

- Score 3: 6.9%

## Conclusion

It would appear as if student support for pre-lecture videos and livestreams is strong. For pre-lecture videos, 72.7% of students gave a score of 5 or more in terms of desire. That means that almost three quarters of the students feel that these videos would have a positive impact on their learning experience. For livestreams, 73.6% of students gave a score of 5 or more. Based on this evidence, it seems that both livestreams and pre-lecture videos would be very beneficial for the learning experience of the students. If it could be implemented without too great of a cost, I think that it would be well worth the COMP department's time to look into this.
